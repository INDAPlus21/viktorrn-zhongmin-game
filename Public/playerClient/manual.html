The Rulebook

Certain cards come with certain attributes, printed on the card as either a symbol or an acronym. The following is a list of existing attributes and explanations of how they work.

Rush

This card attacks immediately on the same turn it's placed.

Flying

This card ignores any opposing cards (unless they have Flying or High Block) and attacks the enemy player directly. It can also only be damaged by Flying or Reach cards.

High Block

This card prevents opposing Flying cards from attacking the player directly.

Reach

This card can attack opposing Flying cards.

Mirror

When this card is played, it will copy all attributes and statistics of the opposing card. It has 2 attack and 2 health if there is no opposing card.

Drunk

This card's attack is randomized between 0 and 3 (inclusive) each time it attacks.

Shield

This card will ignore the first attack it receives, no matter the damage it would've dealt.

Valiant Heart

While this card is on your side of the board, all your Human-type cards gain a shield at the start of your turn.

Marching

After attacking, this card moves into the adjacent space in the specified direction, or changes direction if there is no vacant space. The starting direction is right.

Hound Master

While this card is on your side of the board, you receive one Blood Beast at the start of your turn.

Headed Hunter

This card gains +1 attack for every health the player lost.

Rebirth

This card does not perish when sacrificed, but loses 1 health instead. (NOTE: The card can only be sacrificed once per turn, even if it has more health.)

Sleeve Trick

When this card is played, its owner immediately draws a card from their deck (if there are any to draw).

Trapper

When an enemy card is played in front of this card, the enemy card immediately takes 1 damage.

Retribution

After an enemy card attacks this card, the enemy card takes 1 damage.

Dominance

The opposing enemy card loses 1 attack.

Bleeding

This card loses 1 health at the end of its owner's turn, no matter what.

Unworthy

Sacrificing this card gives no blood.
