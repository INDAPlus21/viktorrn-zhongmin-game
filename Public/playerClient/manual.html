The Rulebook

Certain cards come with certain attributes, printed on the card as either a symbol or an acronym. The following is a list of existing attributes and explanations of how they work.

RS - Rush

This card attacks immediately on the same turn it's placed.

FL - Flying

This card ignores any opposing cards (unless they have Flying or High Block) and attacks the enemy player directly. It can also only be attacked by Flying or Reach cards.

HB - High Block

This card prevents opposing Flying cards from attacking the player directly.

RH - Reach

This card can attack opposing Flying cards.

MR - Mirror

This card will copy all attributes and statistics of the opposing card when played. It has 2 attack and 2 health if there is no opposing card.

DR - Drunk

This card's attack is randomized between 0 and 3 (inclusive) each time when it attacks.

SH - Shield

This card will ignore the first incoming damage it takes.

VH - Valiant Heart

As long as this card is on the board, all other cards on the same side gain a shield at the start of the player's turn.

MA - Marching

After attacking, this card moves to the adjacent space in a certain direction, or changes direction if there is no vacant space. The starting direction is right.

HM - Hound Master

As long as this card is on the board, the player receives a Blood Beast at the start of their turn.

HH - Headed Hunter

This card gains +1 attack for every health the player lost.

RB - Rebirth

Upon sacrificing, this card does not perish, but only loses 1 health.
